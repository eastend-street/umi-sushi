About

Umi Sushi 海鮨

Umi Sushi is a Japanese traditional sushi restaurant in Vancouver. Through continuous improvement and evolution, Umi Sushi aims to offer delicious and creative cuisine that is fresh, healthy, made with care, and integrity of ingredients. Our restaurant is in the center of Vancouver and aims to give the better services for the clients.

Events

Preparation Workshop

In this unique event, we are going to learn how to choose the Freshest ingredients. We are also going to teach how is the best way to cut the fish. In the end, We provide you with the best way to seasoning.

Plating Workshop

On this event, we are going to learn together how to plate your dish.
- what plate to use

Serving Workshop
