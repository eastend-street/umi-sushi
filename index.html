About

Umi Sushi is a quick service restaurant committed to offering fresh and healthy Japanese cuisine for those in a hurry and on the go.
Through continuous improvement and evolution, Umi Sushi aims to offer delicious and creative cuisine that is fresh, healthy, made with care, and integrity of ingredients.
We operate over 5 locations across Canada and all of them aims to give the better services for the clients.
